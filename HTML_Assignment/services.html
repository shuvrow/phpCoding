INITIATIVES

  • Initial 180 hours intensive General Training & Track Specific General Training will be arranged by LICT Project through a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with one nationally recognized training institute (like BARD). This training is all about basic soft skills & the Track Specific basic courses.
  • Specialized 140-180 hours Course Training will be held on different specialized training institutions like BCC, BITM, BIM etc.
  • In last Phase, Practical Job Assignment will be on the job training in different companies for 3 months.
